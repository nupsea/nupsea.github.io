# Systems Thinking in Daily Life

Applying engineering principles to organize daily habits and routines.

Just as complex software relies on well-designed, loosely coupled systems to function reliably, our daily routines and habits can benefit from a systems-oriented approach. Instead of relying purely on willpower, designing environment cues and feedback loops makes building positive habits and workflows much more sustainable.
